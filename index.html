What you eat matters.

20% of all energy consumed and up to one quarter of all greenhouse gases come from making the food we eat. Different foods require vastly different amounts of energy and greenhouse gas emissions, so your choice matters.

Project



Veganali is a short documentary capturing the first vegan ascent of the highest point of North America, Denali. Our mission is:

    I) communicate the scientific consensus calling for a radical change in our food system

    II) empower viewers to make informed decisions about their food consumption
